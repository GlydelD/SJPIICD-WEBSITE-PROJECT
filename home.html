In a celebration aimed at strengthening the nursing community and empowering its future practitioners, the College of Nursing Department of St. John Paul II College of Davao hosted its highly anticipated "Nursing Day 2024" today.

In a spectacular showcase of cultural heritage, the Siningtala Dance Company from St. John Paul II College of Davao dazzled the audience at Miss Earth 2024 with their immersive and colorful performance.

The Office of Student Affairs, in collaboration with the Supreme Student Council, hosted a two-part seminar led by guest speaker Mr. Noli Dimaandal on the first part of the seminar.

The Life Savers Organization of St. John Paul II College of Davao recently conducted a two-day seminar aimed at equipping its members with fundamental life-saving skills.

Rodriguez, Dean of Student Affairs while the oath-taking of officers was led by Geraldine May Capote, SSC Vice-President on September 4, 2024, at the school quadrangle.
