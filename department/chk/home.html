COLLEGE OF HUMAN KINETICS

The College of Human Kinetics (CHK) is steadfastly progressing towards the realization of the vision and mission laid down by its founders and continually upheld by its present leaders. These guiding principles are rooted in a deep commitment to the advancement of physical education, well-being, and sports development, with a forward-looking perspective on how these fields can evolve to meet the needs of future generations.
