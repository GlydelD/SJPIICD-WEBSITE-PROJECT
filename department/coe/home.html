ABOUT

COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

The Geodetic Engineering program at St. John Paul II College of Davao trains students in land surveying, mapping, and GIS, combining traditional methods with modern technologies like GPS and satellite imaging. Its curriculum includes geodesy, cartography, photogrammetry, and practical fieldwork. Graduates can take the Geodetic Engineer Licensure Exam and pursue careers in land surveying, urban planning, and environmental management. The college also offers industry collaborations for internships and hands-on learning. Computer Science Engineering program blending technical expertise and values-driven education. The program covers software development, AI, machine learning, and cybersecurity, with hands-on training in modern programming languages like Python and Java. Students benefit from state-of-the-art labs, internships with IT companies, and career guidance, preparing them for roles like software engineer and data analyst.
