ABOUT

COLLEGE OF TEACHERS
EDUCATION

An academic institution dedicated to the development of future educators. It provides students with a comprehensive foundation in teaching, subject knowledge, and classroom skills to help them succeed in various educational environments. The college emphasizes practical training through teaching internships, research opportunities, and collaboration with schools and educational organizations. The College of Teacher Education prepares graduates to become skilled, knowledgeable, and adaptable educators capable of making a positive impact in diverse educational settings.
