ABOUT

COLLEGE OF CRIMINAL JUSTICE
EDUCATION

The College of Criminal Justice Education (CCJE) at Saint John Paul II College of Davao (SJP2CD)** is dedicated to shaping future leaders in law enforcement, criminology, and criminal justice. Known for its comprehensive academic programs, the CCJE focuses on providing students with a strong foundation in criminology, law enforcement procedures, forensic science, and the criminal justice system. The college aims to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical values necessary to excel in the ever-evolving field of criminal justice. With a commitment to excellence, the CCJE prepares students for careers in various law enforcement agencies, legal professions, and related fields, all while fostering a sense of social responsibility and community engagement.
