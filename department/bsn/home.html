ABOUT

COLLEGE OF NURSING

The College of Nursing at St. John Paul II College of Davao, a hub of excellence in healthcare education and training. Our college is dedicated to producing competent, compassionate, and globally competitive nursing professionals. Here, we combine rigorous academics with hands-on clinical experience to prepare students for the dynamic world of healthcare. With state-of-the-art facilities, expert faculty, and a strong focus on holistic care, we aim to nurture not only skilled nurses but also compassionate caregivers who embody the values of integrity, service, and excellence. We take pride in our commitment to shaping the future of healthcare by empowering our students to make a meaningful difference in the lives of others.
