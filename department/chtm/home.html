ABOUT

COLLEGE OF HOSPITALITY AND
TOURISM MANAGAMENET

The College of Hospitality and Tourism Management (CHTM) is a premier academic institution dedicated to delivering exceptional education and training in hospitality, tourism, and related fields. With a focus on preparing students for success in the rapidly expanding global hospitality and tourism sectors, CHTM equips graduates with the knowledge, skills, and hands-on experience necessary to thrive in dynamic, leadership roles. CHTM offers a comprehensive curriculum that spans critical areas such as hotel and restaurant management, event planning, travel and tour operations, and tourism marketing. The college prioritizes experiential learning, providing students with invaluable opportunities for internships, industry exposure, and practical training. This approach ensures that graduates are not only well-versed in theory but also capable of applying their expertise in real-world settings. Supported by a team of dedicated faculty and strengthened by strategic industry partnerships, CHTM is committed to shaping the next generation of leaders who are prepared to meet the evolving challenges and demands of the global marketplace.
