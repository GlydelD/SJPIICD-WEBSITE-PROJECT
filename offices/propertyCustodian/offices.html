PROPERTY CUSTODIAN

A Property Custodian is an individual assigned the responsibility for the physical control and safeguarding of an organization’s assets, which can include equipment, supplies, tools, furniture, machinery, and other tangible property. The custodian ensures that these items are accounted for, maintained in good condition, and used properly for organizational purposes. In a government context, the property custodian often works within the framework of government property management systems to ensure compliance with public sector regulations and ensure taxpayers’ assets are used effectively and efficiently
