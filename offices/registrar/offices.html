SCHOOL OFFICES

Registrar

The Registrar's Office serves as the central hub for student academic records and enrollment services. It is responsible for maintaining and safeguarding academic transcripts, processing enrollment applications, and managing course schedules. The office also facilitates student requests for certifications, diploma releases, and academic verification documents. Dedicated to providing efficient service, the Registrar's Office ensures compliance with institutional policies and government regulations. Open during standard hours, it supports students with registration, course adjustments, and academic planning to ensure a smooth educational journey.
