AD ASTRA EDITORIAL BOARD

Ad Astra is an organization composed of highly skilled and dedicated students who devote their time, talent, and creativity to producing the yearbook for graduating students. These individuals collaborate tirelessly to design a publication that not only captures the essence of the school year but also serves as a cherished keepsake filled with memories, milestones, and accomplishments. Through their commitment to excellence and teamwork, Ad Astra ensures that each yearbook is a reflection of the graduating class's unique journey and legacy.
